About us

The project on shared mobility was undertaken due to a variety of reasons, including environmental concerns, energy footprints, and economical factors. Shared mobility refers to the use of shared vehicles, such as carpooling, ride-sharing, or bike-sharing, which can greatly reduce the carbon footprint and energy usage compared to individual car ownership. Additionally, shared mobility is often more affordable than traditional transportation options, making it a popular choice for people looking to save money. Furthermore, reducing carbon emissions and promoting sustainability is crucial in the fight against climate change, making shared mobility a crucial solution for creating a more environmentally-friendly future.


We and our group members collectively decided to solve the problem of lack of transportation facilities by the idea of shared mobility
